We're Just the best

Streaming Live since the 1990s!

WJTB is NJIT's official college radio station - since before NJIT existed!

WJTB, once known as WNCE, has been the official radio station for at Newark College of Engineering (and NJIT) since 1968!

Students at NJIT and its precursor, NCE, have broadcasted for 55 years over the radio waves and, since the 90s, strictly digitally.
